Oliver Sykes


Oliver Sykes (Ashford, Kent, England, November 20, 1986) is a British-Brazilian vocalist, musician,

and songwriter known for being the lead vocalist of the band Bring Me The Horizon. While in school,

in 2003, he began designing CD compilations and short tracks under the name Quakebeat. He also played in other bands,

including the hip-hop group Womb 2 Da Tomb with his Bring Me The Horizon bandmate Matt Nicholls, with his brother

in the technical death metal band Purple Curto as a drummer and vocalist with Süssmayer Neil Whiteley and Tom Sykes,

and the band under the pseudonym "Olisaurus," which he later used to release solo songs. Months later, in 2004,

he started the band Bring Me The Horizon.
