Bring Me The Horizon was the first signing of the British label 'Thirty Days of Nights Records', with whom they released their first EP 'This Is What the Edge of Your Seat Was Made For', which reached #41 on the UK album charts.

Sempiternal, the fourth studio album by Bring Me The Horizon released on April 1, 2013, peaked at #3 on the UK album charts. Additionally, it won the Kerrang! award for Best Album.

There Is a Hell, Belive Me I've Seen It. There Is a Heaven, Let's Keep It a Secret(2010), is one of the band's most personal albums, where on the track It Nevers Ends, the singer talks about his personal life and fame, with a dark atmosphere.

Throne is the second official single released for the album That's the Spirit(2015). Donde el tecladista Jordan Fish, afirmó para una entrevista en Rolling Stone Keyboardist Jordan Fish stated in an interview with Rolling Stone that the song represents a new musical and emotional space for the band, and it is considered one of their best hits.

The lead single Shadow Moses from the album Sempiternal(2013), is inspired by the game Metal Gear Solid, created by Hideo Kojima, who recruited them 6 years later to include the song in the soundtrack of his new game DEATH STRANDING.

Recently, the band has played one of the biggest events in Europe that Route Resurrection Fest has proposed to date.
